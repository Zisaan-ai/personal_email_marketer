The Complete Overview of Features and Capabilities

1. Multi-Sender & Account Rotation

Seamlessly integrate multiple SMTP accounts. Choose Automatic Load Balancing (Round-Robin) to let the system dynamically rotate senders, or manually select specific sending accounts for targeted campaigns to protect domain reputation.

Go to 'Accounts' > 'Add Account' to add SMTPs. In campaigns, select 'Auto-Rotate' or manually pick specific accounts.

2. Smart Daily Limits & Warmup

Build domain trust effectively. Enable Smart Auto-Warmup to algorithmically scale your daily sending volume, or set Manual Warmup parameters to strictly control the exact number of emails sent per day.

In Sending Accounts, toggle 'Warmup' for automatic scaling, or manually type your preferred daily limit.

3. DKIM, SPF & DMARC Ready

Maximize deliverability. The system supports full domain authentication protocols. Ensure your custom SMTP settings and sender domains are authenticated to bypass spam filters securely.

Add your SMTP servers with proper DNS records configured at your domain registrar.

4. Dynamic Lead Management

Easily manage massive lists. Upload leads in bulk via TXT. Manage invalid emails, handle bounces, and track specific contacts securely to maintain a healthy subscriber base.

Navigate to Lists > Create List > Upload TXT file to populate your audience instantly.

5. Anti-Spam Humanized Delays

Evade rigorous anti-spam algorithms. The system automatically randomizes sending intervals between your configured boundaries. Define the Min and Max delay ranges to mimic real human behavior.

During campaign setup, input minimum and maximum delay in seconds (e.g., 5 to 15 seconds).

6. Analytics & Performance Tracking

Get true 360-degree analytics. The system embeds tracking pixels to securely detect opens and syncs replies efficiently. Monitor campaign metrics in real-time from the intuitive visual dashboard.

Analytics are collected automatically. View your stats on the Dashboard and inside specific Campaign details.

7. Custom Timezone Support

Full global flexibility. Configure your personalized timezone right from your Settings. The automated cron-jobs will reset daily sending limits exactly at midnight in your specific local time, not a hardcoded server time.

Navigate to 'Settings' and update your Timezone dropdown. Limits will auto-reset at 12:00 AM of that region.

8. Spintax & Hyper-Personalization

Avoid duplicate content penalties. Insert Spintax formats like {Hi|Hello|Hey} into your emails, and the engine automatically generates unique variations for every single lead on the fly.

Manually write Spintax {A|B} in the editor. The system auto-replaces these variables during sending.

9. State-of-the-Art Visual Template Engine

Build stunning newsletters instantly. Use the intuitive visual editor, select from pre-built layouts, or switch to the Source Code view to inject your highly-customized HTML & CSS.

Compose campaigns using the rich text editor or click '< >' to manually write HTML/CSS code.

10. Integrated Support Ticket System

Get help instantly. The built-in ticketing system allows users to seamlessly request support, upload attachments, and track statuses (Open/Closed), while admins can manage inquiries via an exclusive panel.

Go to 'Support Tickets', click 'Create New Ticket' to submit issues, and chat directly with administrators.

This entire advanced email marketing system was architected, designed, and developed by the developer. To ensure enterprise-grade performance and maximum security, it features an extremely powerful and secure admin dashboard, exclusively reserved for administrators. It utilizes JWT token-based authentication, strict RBAC (Role-Based Access Control), and deep encryption algorithms. The system's background automation, cron-jobs, server load balancing, user timezones, and database integrity are monitored and controlled directly from this core dashboard. Along with the facility to manually override in any emergency, this multi-layered security infrastructure ensures user data protection and 100% platform stability.
